# BalanceSphere Product Requirements

## Vision

BalanceSphere visualizes geopolitical actors and their relationships as an interactive 3D network used for analysis, storytelling, scenario-building, and research support. The application is explicitly decision-support oriented and must surface uncertainty rather than imply certainty.

## MVP Scope

- Focus on a Middle East conflict network before expanding globally.
- Represent countries, governments, military actors, proxy groups, regions, strategic assets, and international organizations.
- Show relationship type, direction, strength, sentiment, volatility, confidence, and evidence.
- Support manual data seeding and human-reviewed draft claims.
- Provide a timeline with seeded historical snapshots.
- Use transparent rule-based trend estimates rather than opaque prediction models.

## Product Goals

- Render actors as visually distinct 3D bubbles.
- Separate states from internal or affiliated actors.
- Show weighted, directional, typed relationships.
- Animate historical change through a timeline.
- Ingest structured data and evidence-backed claims.
- Preserve confidence, source count, and recency metadata on every claim-derived output.
- Support scenario simulation without overwriting historical truth.

## Primary Users

- Sci-fi writers and worldbuilders
- Researchers and educators
- Journalists and analysts
- Strategy and documentary creators

## MVP Constraints

- No autonomous publishing from AI extraction.
- No black-box geopolitical forecasting.
- Every score must carry confidence and evidence context.
- Security-sensitive or operational military recommendation behavior is out of scope.
